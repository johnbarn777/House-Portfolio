Executive Summary

House House is a mobile-first collaboration platform built on React-Native just for shared households. It brings everything roommates need into one place—group chats organized by topic (rent, chores, groceries, maintenance), shared task lists with automatic reminders and turn-assignments, and built-in expense-splitting so everyone always knows who owes what. Calendar syncing and quick polls make coordinating appointments, cleaning rotas or weekend outings a breeze, and real-time notifications keep the whole house on the same page. By replacing scattered texts, sticky notes and awkward check-ins with a sleek, intuitive hub, House turns everyday friction into seamless teamwork—so you can spend less time arguing over whose turn it is and more time enjoying your home.
